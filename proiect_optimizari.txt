## вљ™пёЏ AGOA Allocation Logic (`core/agoa_engine.py`)

Implement the following tiered allocation model, evaluated every recomputation cycle:

```
TOP 10%    в†’ receive 50% of total portfolio capital   (Elite tier)
MIDDLE 70% в†’ receive 40% of total portfolio capital   (Core tier)
BOTTOM 10% в†’ receive 10% of total portfolio capital   (Survival tier)
```

- With 10 strategies: Top 1 = Elite, Middle 7 = Core, Bottom 2 = Survival.
- Within each tier, capital is distributed proportionally to composite scores.
- If a strategy has a negative composite score, cap its allocation at the tier minimum.
- The AGOA ranking window is the **last 3 weeks of trading data**.
- Recomputation schedule: **Wednesday 09:31 ET** and **Friday 16:01 ET** (after close).
- After each recomputation, log the allocation change and reason in `outputs/logs/`.

## рџ“Љ Performance Scoring Metric (Composite Score)

Each strategy is scored using:

```
composite_score = (0.5 Г— sharpe_ratio) + (0.3 Г— net_return_pct) в€’ (0.2 Г— max_drawdown_pct)
```

- `sharpe_ratio`: annualized, computed on daily P&L over the 3-week window
- `net_return_pct`: % return over the 3-week window
- `max_drawdown_pct`: worst peak-to-trough drawdown over the 3-week window (positive number = worse)
- All inputs should be normalized to [0, 1] range before weighting, to prevent scale dominance.

**Also track but do not include in ranking by default (expose as config toggle):**
- Win rate
- Profit factor
- Volatility
- Average trade duration
- Calmar ratio

## рџ”Ѓ Simulation Runner (`core/simulation_runner.py`)

- Simulation period: **6 months** (configurable start/end date in `config.py`, default: 6 months before today)
- Data granularity: **1-day OHLCV bars** from `yfinance` (use 1h bars if available for intraday strategies, fall back to daily if not)
- Each strategy runs its `run_day()` method for each trading day in sequence
- Capital is **not shared between strategies** вЂ” each gets its allocated slice; P&L is tracked separately per strategy then aggregated
- Reallocation events fire on Wednesdays and Fridays; the 3-week window lookback must always reference actual trading days (not calendar days)
- After all 6 months, call `report_generator.py` to produce the final output

### Suggested Additional Dynamic Indicators to Integrate:

1. **VIX Regime Filter**: When market VIX > 25, automatically increase Survival tier floor to 5%, reduce Elite ceiling to 40%. Protects against fragile strategies in chaotic markets.
2. **Momentum Confirmation**: Before assigning Elite status, check if the strategy's 5-day rolling return is still positive (trend confirmation).
3. **Inter-Strategy Correlation Penalty**: If two strategies have >0.85 return correlation over the lookback period, penalize the lower-scoring one's composite score by 15% to encourage diversification.
4. **Sector Concentration Cap**: Strategies trading the same sector ETFs cannot collectively exceed 35% of total capital.
5. **Drawdown Circuit Breaker**: Any strategy with rolling drawdown > 20% in the current window is automatically demoted to Survival tier regardless of score.
6. **Adaptive Lookback**: In high-volatility environments (realized vol > 1.5Г— 90-day avg), extend lookback window from 3 to 5 weeks to smooth signal noise.
7. **Win Rate Floor**: If a strategy's win rate drops below 35% in the last 10 trades, apply a composite score penalty of 20%.
8. **Trade Frequency Normalizer**: Normalize scores by number of trades to prevent strategies with very few trades from receiving artificially high Sharpe ratios.
